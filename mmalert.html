    • MMAlert

    As a big fan of sports and live events, I personally find it hard to keep track of live sporting events regarding the sports that I care enough about to watch live. Other mainstream sports have several capable apps serving this purpose, but MMA does not have any such apps.

    MMAlert is a personal project that allows MMA fanatics to keep track of live events occurring within various regional and global mixed martial arts promotions.

    Due to issues within the app reviewing process that involve copyright legalities, the app is not currently out in the public app store. The plan is to eventually outfit the app appropriately for public release. The slideshow to the right presents advertisements made for its public release, pictures taken in-app.
